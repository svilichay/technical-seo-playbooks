# AI Search Optimization Playbook

Framework for optimizing websites and content for AI-driven discovery systems including ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and AI Overviews.

---

## Core Principles

AI systems prioritize:

- Clear entity definitions
- Structured content
- Authoritative sources
- Concise answers
- Semantic relationships between topics

---

## Content Optimization

Best practices:

- Lead sections with direct answers
- Use clear definitions
- Structure content with headings
- Break complex topics into scannable sections
- Include supporting examples

---

## Entity Optimization

Ensure search engines and AI systems clearly understand:

- Brand entities
- Product entities
- Regulatory entities
- Industry terminology

Use:

- structured data
- internal linking
- glossary content

---

## Structured Data

Recommended schema types:

- Organization
- Article
- Product
- FAQ
- WebPage

Structured data helps machines extract key information.

---

## Internal Linking

Reinforce topic relationships through:

- glossary linking
- product linking
- topic clusters
- regulatory references

---

## Measurement

Monitor:

- AI citations
- AI referral traffic
- AI answer inclusion
- SERP feature visibility
